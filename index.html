About Me

I was born in New Jersey and shortly after moved to Hong Kong where I spent all of my younger years until I graduated Lafayette College in 2019 with a degree in Engineering and a minor in Economics. I then received my Masters of Science in International Management from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, and worked as a Private Credit Analyst. While this was enjoyable and very educational, it lacked the fulfillment and joy I sought after, eventually leading me to pursue a career in Software Development.

The best way I thought to accomplish this goal was to enroll in App Academy, a six month long full stack coding bootcamp where I learned many relevant front-end and back-end skills. Fast forward to now and I can confidently say that I made the right choice of pursuing this career. And while I learned so much over the last half year or so, I know that there are still so many more skills and technologies out there to learn.

In my free time, I enjoy taking my 7 year old Golden Doodle Charlie on walks, researching the newest PC parts, and spending quality time with friends and family. In the winter months, I love to chase storms and ski whenever I can. Check out some of my skiing videos here!
